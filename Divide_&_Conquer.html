T-SUMMIT



Time to unite your ideas, innovation, presence of mind, coordination and everything else that helps in marking your identity! A mock industry with realistic obstacles that you must overcome.

PROCEDURE:
As you register yourself, we will assign you any one of the group - investor, business head or marketing head as per your preference.
Also each group would be linked to two other groups (consisting of people of the remaining two categories). Each group would be given restricted situations with several obstacles of the respective field.

WHAT TO DO:
The objective is to overcome the situation in such a manner that it not only benefits the group itself but also be helpful to the linked groups. Finally all three linked groups (investors +business heads +marketing heads) must merge and present a complete output. A well established industry with minimal loop holes and maximum innovations!

JUDGING CRITERIA:
You will be judged upon every decision and step taken, by professionals. Team work plays a crucial role in this activity. The best team will be awarded a cash prize or conference passes* with a merit certificate. All the participants will be awarded a participation certificate.
*The Conference will be on 22nd Feb at Institution of Engineers Khairatabad.
