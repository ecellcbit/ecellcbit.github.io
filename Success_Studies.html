T-SUMMIT



Let's see how best you are at data analytics! Compile and study a company that is allotted to you in depth including its profile, philosophy, history and rise, board members, policies, present marketing strategy, future scope, competitors and innovations and why it stands out to you. Be creative with how you project your company!

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:
The studies should be compiled in two documents:
Document 1: A document containing the profile, philosophy, history and rise, board members, policies, competitors, the past and present situation of the firm and its market presence and strategy.
Document 2: A document containing your views on the future scope of the firm, innovations and why it stands out to you.

NOTE:
• Anyone can enroll into the competition by filling the Google sheet. (The company would be emailed to them)
• The competition will be for teams or individuals.
• The team can have a maximum of 4 participants.

ROUNDS:
The first round will have submission of the documents compiled by the participants.
The second and final round will have participants presenting their findings in a 5 minute presentation in front of judges.
The scoring of the final round will be based on the quality of report, presentation skills; ability to respond to questions regarding that industry/firm in and around the world and how his/her company plays a role.
The team which finishes the game with the highest points will be termed as the winner and will be awarded a cash prize or conference passes* with a merit certificate. All the participants will be awarded a participation certificate.
*The Conference will be on 22 nd Feb at Institution of Engineers Khairatabad.
