T-SUMMIT
I M P R E S A R I O S Y M P O S I U M

(CONFERENCE)



The third day of the T-SUMMIT 2K17 would be a me`lange of some of the best Entrepreneurs of the country, speaking on several topics such as Engineering and Entrepreneurship, Ideation process, Technology Ventures, Innovation, Venture Capital etc.

The theme of the conference, "Entrepreneurship and Innovation: A Path for Growth and Development" aims to bring papers containing original contributions on theories, practice, empirical studies, case analysis and researches in all areas of entrepreneurship and innovation.

Topics include but not limited to the following:

1. Technology Management and Venture Capital Sourcing i. Supply Chain and Logistics Management
ii. Sourcing of Funds
2. Human Capital Development - Workforce Management
3. Marketing Management i. Alternative Distribution Channels
ii. Trends in Market Development
iii. E-business/ E-commerce
4. Enterprise Development and Education i. Family Business
ii. Innovation in Entrepreneurship Education
iii. Managing Growing Business
