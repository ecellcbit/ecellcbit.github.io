Technopreneurial summit 2017 is the first ever national level 3-day technology entrepreneurship event of CBIT. It is a celebration of technology based entrepreneurship. The Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) of CBIT has set the ball rolling with the first ever entrepreneurship event to be conducted in the institute.  It is a three day event where in the first two days, the T-SUMMIT organising team and CBIT play host to various events challenging the entrepreneur in participants the second day is the National conference, which provides a forum to all the delegates to learn and interact with eminent entrepreneurs and managers, and a series of entrepreneurial events.

M&A (Mergers And Acquisitions)

(M&A) is a general term that refers to the consolidationof companies or assets. While there are several types of transactions classified under the notion of M&A, a merger means a combination of two companies toform a new company, while an acquisition is the purchase of one company byanother in which no new company is formed.
