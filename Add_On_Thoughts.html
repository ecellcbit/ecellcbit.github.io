T-SUMMIT



Advertisements play an important role when it comes to publishing your product.The more catchy the advertisement the more effective the sales. This event is designed in such a way that it will boost your creativity and memory.

Team Details: 1 or 2 students per team

There are two levels in this competition:
STAGE 1:
Logo quiz
This level is pretty simple. The team will be given the taglines or the logos of the products and they have to guess the product. Every team will be given 2 minutes to guess the taglines/logos and the team will be rewarded with one point for every tagline/logo correctly guessed.
*The teams which guess more than the average score of all the teams will move on to the next round.

STAGE 2:
Build Advertisements
Here every team is given a mock product and they have to come up with catchy advertising ideas and present it.
Every team gets 10 minutes to present their ideas. The team can present their idea in the form of role-play or in any other form which they think would help them in putting forward their idea in best way. The teams will be judged on the following criteria.

CRITERIA FOR JUDGEMENT:
1. Relevance
2. Creativity
3. Language proficiency
4. Originality
5. Overall presentation
The team which finishes the game with the highest points will be termed as the winner and will be awarded a cash prize or conference passes* with a merit certificate. All the participants will be awarded a participation certificate.
*The Conference will be on 22 nd Feb at Institution of Engineers Khairatabad.

Registration fee:
INR 100 for a team of 1
INR 150 for a team of 2
